FULL LIST OF CONTROLS:

Lighting Movements:
Q: Move light -x
q: Move light +x
W: Move light +y
w: Move light -y
E: Move light +z
e: Move light -z

Lighting Modes (Ambient/Diff/Toon):
T: Enable toon shading
t: Switch back to Phong

A/a: Ambient only
S/s: Ambient + Diff + Specular
D/d: Diffuse only

Heirarchical Movements:
Z: Rotate clockwise y
z: Rotate counter-clockwise y
X: Rotate clockwise x
x: Rotate counter-clockwise x
C: Rotate clockwise z
c: Rotate counter-clockwise z

G/g: Tetra y rotation
Y/y: Tetra z rotation
U/u: Tetra x rotation

H: Rotate cone/sphere/tetra clockwise y
h: Rotate cone/sphere/tetra counter-clockwise y

Camera Rotations:
J: Look right
j: Look left
I: Look up
i: Look down

Translations:
K: Translate down
k: Translate up
L: Translate left
l: Translate right

Scaling:
M: Scale down
m: Scale up
